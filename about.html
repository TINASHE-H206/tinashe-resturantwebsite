Our Story

Discover the passion behind Savory Elegance

Culinary Excellence Since 2005

Founded by Tinashe Hendrick, our restaurant has been a beacon of fine dining in the city for over 15 years. What began as a small 30-seat establishment has grown into an award-winning destination for food enthusiasts.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients, treat them with respect, and let their natural flavors shine. We work closely with local farmers, fishermen, and artisans to bring you the freshest seasonal offerings.

In 2019, we were honored with the prestigious Freedmore Sedume, a testament to our team's dedication to culinary artistry and exceptional service.
